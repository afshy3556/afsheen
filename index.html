Services

Hospitality practice

  • Level 2 Certificate in Hospitality Practice: A practical skills qualification that prepares students for their first job in hospitality. It covers a range of hospitality roles, including front of house, housekeeping, and restaurant and bar service.
  • Hospitality Practice skills programs: Designed to help address skills shortages in the hospitality sector.

Culinary arts

  • Level 2 Diploma in Culinary Skills: A qualification that builds on the skills learned at the award and certificate level. It introduces dishes and techniques that require a higher level of skill, such as pastries, desserts, and cakes.

  • Level 4 Diploma in Culinary Arts: An advanced qualification that prepares students to work in a professional kitchen.

Front of house service

  • Level 2 Certificate in Professional Restaurant Front of House Service: A qualification that teaches students how to create the best customer experience. It covers customer service styles, drink pairings, and how to manage customer complaints.
